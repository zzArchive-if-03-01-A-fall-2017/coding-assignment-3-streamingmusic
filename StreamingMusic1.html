STREAMING MUSIC

Definition of streaming music

Streaming is a technology used to deliver content to computers and mobile devices over the internet. The difference between downloading and streaming is that data that you download is permanently stored on your device, as opposed to data you stream, which is automatically deleted and so you don't have to wait for it to be completely downloaded before you use it.
